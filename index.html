WHO AM I?

Zone 6 born and raised, so Omeretta the Great is not excluding me. Growing up so deep in the city allowed me to see all sides of Atlanta, and meet all of its different people. A city in the trees, and a populous so diverse, it is impossible not to fall in love. After graduating from Grady High School (now known as Midtown High) I joined the Georgia Army National Guard to assist in paying for college at the University of North Georgia. I received a Bachelors of Science in Criminal Justice and a Paralegal Certificate from the University of Georgia.

WHAT AM I?

Graduate of the Vets Who Code program. This bootcamp has provided me strong knowledge of GitHub, HTML, CSS, the princibles of UI/UX, Javascript and now React. Pushing forward I am always seeking to practice what I have learned and gain more skills!

Why am I?

Although my initial plan was to work in the legal field, as many mid-twenties and older people know, things don't always go according to plan. After five years in the legal field I decided it was time to move on. It was not what I expected, and not an environment I personally thrive in. Tech, on the other hand, has always been an interest of mine, and after talking over with friends who are on all different sides from management to back end, it was time for me to make the jump to something that truly interested me, and pushes me to become better.
